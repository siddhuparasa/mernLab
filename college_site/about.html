About Us

Established in 1990, Prasad V. Potluri Institute of Technology is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in technical education and research. We aim to foster a robust learning environment for our students, blending cutting-edge technology with practical learning experiences.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and ethical practices, preparing students to contribute to society and lead with confidence.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in technical education by nurturing talent, encouraging research, and building partnerships with industry leaders.

30+ Years of Excellence

We have been a pioneer in providing quality technical education since our inception.

Modern Campus

Our campus is equipped with cutting-edge labs, libraries, and collaborative spaces.

Global Network

Our alumni work in leading organizations across 50+ countries worldwide.
